Filter the dataset using the filter options below. After you made a selection, the treemap will automatically update. The treemap shows only one hierarchical level at the time, with an indication of the next hierarchical level. Click on a node in the treemap to zoom into that nodes next hierarchical level. You can zoom out by clicking on the breadcrumb bar, which shows the zoomed in nodes. Hovering over a node shows the total size of that node compared to the overall total size.

The estimates for the total populations of the continents for each of the cross section years, were derived from the dataset "Statistics on World Population, GDP and Per Capita GDP, 1-2008 AD" by Angus Maddison (http://www.ggdc.net/maddison/Historical_Statistics/horizontal-file_02-2010.xls).

The estimates for 1650 and 1800 were calculated by using the linear growth between 1600 & 1700 and 1700 & 1820 respectively. The ordering of the countries in the original dataset was readjusted to align them with the continents used in this treemap application.
